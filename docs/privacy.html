Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Lindra is designed to help users log and review migraine information. The app stores migraine entries locally on the device.

Data Collection

Lindra does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal data on remote servers as part of the core app experience.

Local Storage

Migraine entries and related details are stored locally on the user’s device using on-device storage.

Sharing

If a user chooses to copy or export a report, that action is initiated by the user. Lindra does not automatically share health information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@wiklander.org.
